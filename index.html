How to play

  1. Press "space" or "tap" to hit inside the target area
  2. Hit outside of the target and you lose 5 points
  3. Every success hit inside the target area adds 25 points
  4. Hit either end and you lose 10 points
  5. Start the game by pressing "space" or "tap"

Press ESC key or click outside to close
